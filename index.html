WELCOME TO MY WORLD

Vishwajit Sharma

a Motivational Speaker Entrepreneur Human Trainer

Being a hardcore Entrepreneur, I always strive for more in my life. I believe in the principle of abundance which says that - "Life is full of abundance. We just need to ask from it and do the karma." If you keep on doing the same things again and again, you'll get the same result again and again. Therefore, we should always try to do different things all the time. With this philosophy in my mind, I started my entrepreneurial journey at a very young age while I was in my college. In the last 9 years of my journey, I was fortunate to get the company of the most successful people of my age. Also, I could able to help and guide thousands of youngsters to discover their paths and ultimately fulfilling their dreams. I feel blessed that God chose me and my sessions as a medium to get transformation in the lives of youngsters, especially students. With the same Truthfulness, Honesty, Kind-heartedness, Love & Passion, I will try to spread the air of positivity and change in the lives of whosoever comes to me.

About Me

Hello, I am Vishwajit Sharma

Being a meritorious student I completed my schooling from different parts of the country. I pursued my graduation in Mechanical Engineering from Sinhgad College Of Engineering(Vadgaon), Pune University. Coming from an para military background, competency and discipline was part of my modus operandi from the early days itself. By the grace of God, I had been hitting the nail on the head whenever I participated in any competition during school or college days. In a quest to achieve more, I started my entrepreneurial career while being an engineering student. Subsequently, I could become an eminent public speaker and human trainer.

Some of the notable achievements of my career so far are:-

  1. I have been invited on several occasions in different cities like Nagpur, Pune, Nashik, Mumbai, etc. to address the youngsters in various Youth Empowerment Events.
  2. I was invited by Sinhgad College (Kondhwa, Pune) to conduct a workshop on Interpersonal and Entrepreneurial Skill Development.
  3. I have been recognized as the distinguished alumni by Sinhgad college of engineering, Vadgaon, Pune.
  4. I was one of the keynote speakers to deliver the talk on entrepreneurship, at one of the largest indoor stadiums of Maharashtra i.e. Balewadi Stadium, Pune.
  5. In the past 9 years, I have delivered my talks across various cities of the country addressing youth on the topic of personality development, personal branding and entrepreneurship.
